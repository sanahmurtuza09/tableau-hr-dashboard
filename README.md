# Human Resources Analytics Dashboard (Tableau)

An interactive HR analytics dashboard built in Tableau Public to explore workforce composition, compensation trends, and employee lifecycle metrics.

🔗 **Live Dashboard:**  
https://public.tableau.com/app/profile/sanah.murtuza/viz/HRDashboard_17688351295730/SummaryDashboard

---

## Project Overview

This dashboard provides both an executive-level summary and a detailed employee view of HR data. It is designed to help stakeholders quickly analyze workforce trends, demographic distribution, performance insights, and salary patterns.

---

## Business Insights

- 7,984 active employees with 966 terminations indicate moderate workforce turnover.
- The 35–44 age group represents the largest workforce segment (29%), suggesting a mid-career dominant population.
- Operations and Sales are the largest departments, reflecting operational intensity.
- Salary progression correlates strongly with age and managerial roles.
- Education level shows visible performance distribution differences.
- Workforce distribution clusters around HQ and key branch locations.

These insights can support retention strategy, compensation planning, and workforce forecasting.
